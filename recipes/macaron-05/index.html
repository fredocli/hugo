CHOCOLATE GANACHE

For about 40 pcs. macarons

INGREDIENTS

  • 250 g whipping cream
  • 200 g dark chocolate 67% cocoa
  • 20 g unsalted butter

PREPARATION

Chop the chocolate coarsely and put it into a bowl. Bring whipping cream to a boil. Pour a third of the boiling cream into the chocolate and stir gently with a rubber spatula. Add the rest of the liquid by two times and gently stir with a rubber spatula until the ganache is uniform and homogeneous. Add the butter when the ganache is between 40°C and 45°C. Stir in the ganache until the butter is melted. Cover the ganache with a piece of plastic film and put it in the refrigerator.
