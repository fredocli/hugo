# STREUSEL

## INGREDIENTS


- 50 g caster sugar
- 50 g salted butter
- 75 g wheat flour
- 25 g almond flour

## PREPARATION

Mix flour, almond flour and sugar in a bowl.
Crumble the butter into the mixture with your fingers until the dough collects and you can form small chunk.
