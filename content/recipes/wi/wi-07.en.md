# GLAZE

## INGREDIENTS


- 150 g icing sugar
- 50 g glucose syrup
- 25 g boiling water

## PREPARATION

Stir in icing sugar, glucose syrup and boiling water to form a thick glaze.
Put the glaze in a piping bag with a small tip.
